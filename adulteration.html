What is Food Adulteration?

Adding unwanted or harmful substances to food to increase quantity or improve appearance, often for profit. These additives—called adulterants—are dangerous to health.

Types of Adulteration:

  • Intentional Adulteration
  • Incidental Adulteration
  • Metallic Adulteration
